About Game Rewind UK
A small nostalgia time machine built around gaming and UK culture.
What it does

Search for a game, or browse by date or console, and Game Rewind UK shows what else was happening in the same month and year:

  • Games released in the UK
  • Films in UK cinemas
  • What was available to rent
  • Top 40 singles that month
  • What wrestling event was happening that month
Where the data comes from

The app loads its data from a Google Sheet, so updates to the sheet appear in the app automatically. A lot of the release information has been sourced manually because UK dates are often inconsistently recorded.

How to use it
  • Search: start typing a game title and pick from suggestions.
  • Browse by date: choose a month and year to see that release window.
  • Browse by console: pick a console and view games A-Z.
  • Multiple versions: if a game appears on more than one console, you'll be asked to choose the one you meant.
Notes

This is a personal project. Some months may have incomplete data, and titles may vary depending on how they're written in the database. I have tried to be as true and accurate as I can be.

If you spot a mistake, or have some knowledge about a game title or similar then please contact me at gamerewinduk@gmail.com
